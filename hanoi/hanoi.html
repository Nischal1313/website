Try to get the pyramid to the left/the right side.

Rules:

  1. Smaller on Top: A smaller disk can be placed on top of a larger disk, but not vice versa.
  2. One Disk at a Time: Only one disk can be moved at a time.
  3. From Top of a Stack: A disk can only be moved from the top of one pyramid to the top of another pyramid.
  4. Think about every move carefully. Five invalid moves will reset the game.
